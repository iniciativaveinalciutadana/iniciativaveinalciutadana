Who We Are

We are a group of neighbors from the Sant Antoni neighborhood in Barcelona, united by the desire to care for, improve, and defend our environment. We are actively involved in addressing the problems that affect our daily life: insecurity, urban decay, drug and alcohol use in public spaces, nighttime disturbances, and lack of social attention to people in SITUATIONS OF VULNERABILITY/SOCIAL EXCLUSION.

Our initiative emerged due to the lack of response from the City Council in the face of the neighborhoodâ€™s degradation, which was affecting our quality of life. We decided to take the first step: gather signatures to request more attention and solutions from the City Council and the Mossos d'Esquadra (police).

On June 19, 2025, we presented these signatures, and soon more neighbors from different streets joined with their own concerns. Although the City Council has started an action plan, progress is too slow, and we need them to act with more urgency.

The civic neighbors' initiative is nonpartisan and based on responsible action.
We seek humane, fair, and effective solutions FOR ALL THE INVOLVED PARTIES.

We are not a formal association nor a political party: we are people who share streets, squares, and neighborhood life, convinced that only together can we achieve positive change.

Invitation to Join the Initiative

We want to invite you to join this civic initiative, which we have launched as a group of neighbors concerned about the problems we face every day in the neighborhood: drug and alcohol use in public spaces, violence, threats and intimidation of neighbors, street parties, nighttime noise and disturbances, lack of cleanliness and urban decay, limited police presence, inefficient social policies, and neglect of people in vulnerable situations/exclusion.

We are residents of Sant Antoni who denounce the degradation of our neighborhood and want to recover it. We want a safer, cleaner, more dignified, and more supportive neighborhood for everyone.

We are here to demand that our City Council and the relevant institutions act with URGENCY, DETERMINATION, AND COMMITMENT to effectively and coordinatedly provide solutions to the problems we are facing.

What Do We Ask For?

  • Street cleaning and care for public spaces.
  • Fines for citizens who do not respect the common space.
  • Security: more police presence to guarantee it.
  • Social solutions for homeless people: housing, health, and support. Some of these individuals suffer from mental health issues and should receive personalized attention and help.
  • That neither the neighbors nor the most vulnerable people be forgotten.

Why Do We Sign?

We sign to formally request from the City Council:

  • To stop ignoring the situation.
  • To act with commitment, firmness, and humanity.
  • To take care of our neighborhood and all the people who live in it.

This is a peaceful and voluntary civic initiative. Together, we can recover a safe, clean, and dignified Sant Antoni for everyone. We count on you to build a better neighborhood together.


Documents Submitted

  • Three petitions addressed to the City Council and the Mossos d'Esquadra, requesting coordinated institutional intervention with a focus on security, public health, and social support.
  • Signature sheets collected from neighbors, presented voluntarily, peacefully, and responsibly, as an expression of concern for the common good.

We hope that this documentation will be considered with the seriousness it deserves, and we are available to collaborate in any dialogue or institutional forum that can help find real solutions.
